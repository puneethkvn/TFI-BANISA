Captain America: Brave New World Movie Review

Directed by: Julius Onah

Starring: Anthony Mackie, Harrison Ford, Shira Haas

Release Date: May 3, 2025

Rating: 8.5/10

Plot Summary

"Captain America: Brave New World" sees Sam Wilson take up the mantle of Captain America in a new era. As the world grapples with new threats and a shifting global power structure, Sam must confront a new villain who challenges the very idea of what it means to be a hero.

Performance

Anthony Mackie shines in his first full movie as Captain America, bringing a refreshing and heartfelt performance. His chemistry with the supporting cast, including Harrison Ford as General Thaddeus Ross, adds layers of complexity to the film.

Conclusion

"Captain America: Brave New World" is an exciting, action-packed film that introduces a new chapter in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. With great performances, a compelling story, and an ever-expanding world, it’s a must-watch for MCU fans.
